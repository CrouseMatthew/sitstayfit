Classes

Classes will combine cardio with strength training elements, working on all major muscle groups to help you achieve your fitness goals. While your work out is important to us, so is the dog training part! We will work on name recognition, stay, tricks and more, all during your workout.

We meet at various parks around Portland, and are always looking to try out somewhere new. Want us to hold a class at a park near you, that you don’t see listed below? Let us know!

Mood

Regular exercise improves your mood, self esteem and increases your energy. It also decreases your stress level. Your dog will reap the benefits as well.

Health

Improved Health for you & your dog

Behavior

Lack of exercise is one of the leading causes of behavior problems in dogs, and our class will provide you with the confidence that you have met your dog's physical and mental stimulation needs.

Attention

Our positive reinforcement training style will help your dog learn to pay better attention to you around distractions.

Bonding

Our classes will strengthen the bond between you and your dog.

Need More Help With Your Dog?
Let Us Do The Work!

Our Board & Train program is a perfect option if you have a puppy that is testing your patience, exhibiting early warning signs of a behavior problem, or if you simply don’t have the time to work on the puppy basics due to vacation or a long work day.

Leave your pup in the hands of a Certified Professional Dog Trainer, who will ensure that the training important to you is being done in an efficient and compassionate way, that will give you the results you want. With our positive based methods we will work with things like play biting, house training, basic obedience cues, as well as confidence building and impulse control, as it applies.

Not only will you be able to feel confident that your pup is in great hands, but you’ll come home to a happy, tired and better trained dog.

Want to leave your pup with us just for the day?

We have day training available, where we will squeeze all this in, and bring your pup home in the evening. More information here.

Our Overnight Package Includes:

  • Overnight stay at a certified trainer's house - guaranteed.
  • Pick Up and Drop Off Included
  • At least one trianing outing per day with opportunity for socialization (vaccinations permitting). We will provide opportunities such as a "happy" visit to your vet, walks in a busy neighborhod, settle practice at a restaurant, and more
  • 1+ hours spent in supervised play and interactions with appropriate, playful adult dogs, ensuring socialization, boundaries in play and bite inhibition lessons.
  • 1+ hours spent in training sessions working on your choice of training items. If none selected, we will cover things like not jumping on people, coming when called, go to bed on cue and loose leash walking.
  • Separation training, where the pup will spent time safely separated from us to prevent the development of separation anxiety. During these times, with your permission, we may provide them with puzzle toys, kongs, etc.
  • 15 minute follow up and detailed written progress report, including homework and overview of training progress upon drop off.
  • During longer stays, we can provide regular email updates (photos included!) to help keep you at ease while your puppy is in our care.
  • 1 hour private training included in each stay longer than 5 nights.
Overnight Stay: $165/night (24 hour period) Discounts Available after 5 sessions- ask us for info.

Walk & Runs

Does your dog need a workout? Or More Training? Let us do the work!

We aren’t your average dog walker! As trainers, we will focus on helping your dog achieve the skills important to you. Every walk your dog will be learning, making him that much better behaved the next time you walk him.


Exercise is a vital part of keeping your dog healthy and happy. That’s why at Sit Stay Fit, we believe in the importance of exercising your dog, both mentally and physically. You tell us what aspects of training to focus on (such as loose leash walking, leave it around distractions, etc.) and how much exercise your dog needs. Then, we will get it done in a fun, positive way that will result in a better behaved, tired dog.

Whether you have an excitable dog that just needs more exercise, a leash reactive dog that needs training, or you just don’t want your dog to spend the day bored, and home alone- we can help! As trainers, we will employ our positive (and effective!) reward based methods the second we step out your front door. Our service will result in a dog that’s not just tired at the end of the walk, but better trained.

Our Amazing Team

As dog lovers (maybe fanatic is a better word!) we’ve worked with dogs most of our lives. Between growing up around dogs, owning our own dogs, working in dog training and volunteering in shelters, we have tons of combined experience. We’ve always believed in the power of training using positive methods, and the importance of exercise in our personal and professional lives. And after working together in dog training for awhile, we slowly learned ways we could combine these two crucial pieces of dog ownership.

As fitness enthusiasts ourselves, we both truly believe in group fitness. You work harder, you enjoy it more and the class experience keeps you motivated and coming back. Our unique classes, and one of the very first like it in our home town of Portland, will help keep you motivated and leading a healthier lifestyle. We will also help you make sure you are meeting your dog’s exercise needs, as well as help you build better focus from your dog in the presence of distractions. We will even teach some tricks, some for strengthening and stretching, others just for fun!

We believe in a positive, encouraging atmosphere for both dogs and humans, you should feel great when you leave; getting up and out the door to exercise both you and your dog will be one of the best choices you’ve made all day! Our dogs love it, we hope you and yours do too.
